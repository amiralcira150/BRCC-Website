HISTORY
BRCC

BINANGONAN RECREATION AND
CONFERENCE CENTER

Established in 2013, It is one of the monumental projects of Municipal Government of Binangonan, Rizal under the administration of Hon. Cecilio "Boyet" M. Ynares.

BRCC holds the first of its kind in the whole Province of Rizal with a state-of-the-Art Amenities. It was built with a wide range sports and recreational facility suites for small and considerable big numbers of guests.

Later in 2017, it opened a 214sqm kiddie water park and a hotel with 16 units furnished rooms.

Since then, we continually improve and provide high quality of service to the highest form of satisfaction, maintaining excellent relationship with clients.

Customer satisfaction was the sole purpose of BRCC throughout the years, and it will always be for many more years to come.
